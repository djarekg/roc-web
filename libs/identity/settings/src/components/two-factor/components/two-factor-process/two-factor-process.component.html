Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account. In addition to your username and password, you'll need to enter a code generated from a two-factor authentication application on your phone in order to log in.

When you sign into the application, you'll enter your username and password as normal.
You'll then need to enter an additional code from your phone.
Once you enter the code on the website, you'll be logged in.
